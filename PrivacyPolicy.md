# Privacy Policy for DyslexiRead

**Last Updated:** May 1, 2026

DyslexiRead ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome/Edge Extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

### 1. Data Collection
We **do not** collect, store, or sell any personal data, browsing history, or personally identifiable information (PII). 

### 2. How the Extension Works
DyslexiRead modifies the appearance of web pages (fonts, spacing, colors) entirely locally on your device. 
When you use the AI features (such as summarizing text or explaining complex words), the selected text is temporarily sent to our secure backend API exclusively for processing. This text is **not stored, saved, or logged** in any database after the AI finishes processing the request.

### 3. Local Analytics
The extension tracks reading time and words aided to provide you with a Progress Report. This data is stored **locally on your device** within the browser's secure storage. We do not have access to this data.

### 4. Third-Party Services
The backend API relies on AI processing. Data sent to the AI is anonymized and used strictly to generate the requested summary or reading aid in real-time.

### 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the support email provided in the extension store listing.
